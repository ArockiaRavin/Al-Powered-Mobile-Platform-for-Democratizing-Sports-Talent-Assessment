🏅 AI-Powered Mobile Platform for Democratizing Sports Talent Assessment

📌 Abstract

The AI-Powered Mobile Platform for Democratizing Sports Talent Assessment is an innovative solution designed to transform the way sports talent is identified and nurtured. By leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), the platform enables objective, data-driven evaluation of athletes through a mobile application. The system aims to eliminate geographical, financial, and accessibility barriers, ensuring that talented athletes receive equal opportunities for recognition and development.

---

🎯 Problem Statement

Sports talent identification often depends on manual scouting, limited resources, and subjective judgments. As a result, many talented athletes remain undiscovered, particularly in rural and underserved regions.

There is a need for a scalable, affordable, and technology-driven solution that can assess athletic performance fairly and efficiently while providing actionable insights for athletes and coaches.

---

💡 Proposed Solution

This platform utilizes AI and ML algorithms to analyze athlete performance metrics such as speed, agility, endurance, reaction time, and accuracy. The system generates comprehensive performance reports, talent scores, rankings, and personalized training recommendations to support athlete growth and talent discovery.

🏗 System Architecture

Athlete → Mobile Application → Data Collection → AI/ML Processing Engine → Performance Evaluation → Talent Scoring → Analytics Dashboard → Recommendations

---

🔄 Workflow

1. Athlete creates a profile.
2. Performance data is recorded through the mobile application.
3. Data is securely stored and processed.
4. AI models evaluate performance metrics.
5. Talent scores and reports are generated.
6. Coaches and scouts review athlete insights.
7. Personalized recommendations are provided for improvement.
